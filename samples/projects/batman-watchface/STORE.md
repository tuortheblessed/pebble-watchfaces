# Pebble App Store Listing

## App Name
Bat Signal

## Tagline
The Dark Knight watches over Gotham... and your wrist

## Description
Transform your Pebble into the iconic Bat Signal! Watch as the searchlight sweeps across Gotham's night sky, illuminating the legendary bat symbol with a dramatic glow.

**Features:**
- Animated searchlight beam sweeping across the sky
- Iconic Batman logo with pulsing glow effect
- Gotham City skyline silhouette with glowing windows
- Twinkling stars in the night sky
- Clear time and date display
- Battery indicator
- Battery-aware animation (conserves power when low)

Perfect for Batman fans and anyone who wants a dramatic, eye-catching watchface.

Works on all Pebble watches!
